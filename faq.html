Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our solar solutions, installations, and support.

Do your solar systems work during the rainy season? ⌄

Yes. Solar panels generate electricity from daylight, not heat. Even on cloudy or rainy days, your system continues to produce power. Our smart inverters and battery systems ensure consistent energy availability.

How long does installation take? ⌄

Most residential installations are completed within 1–3 days, depending on system size and site conditions. We handle everything from assessment to final testing.

Will solar power my entire home? ⌄

Yes. We design systems based on your energy needs. From essential appliances to full-home coverage, we tailor solutions that fit your lifestyle and budget.

What happens during power outages? ⌄

With battery backup, your solar system continues supplying power during outages. Critical loads remain active, giving you energy independence and peace of mind.

Do you provide maintenance and support? ⌄

Absolutely. We offer ongoing maintenance, system monitoring, and customer support to ensure optimal performance throughout your system’s lifespan.
